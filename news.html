Achievements
  • Nominee for Brain T.Milbrandt Memorial Award for Academic And Professional Excellence

    Nominee for the prestigious Graduate Public Service Internship Brain Memorial Award, which honors exceptional academic and professional excellence. This award is a significant recognition within the academic community, highlighting individuals who have demonstrated outstanding contributions to public service and exemplified leadership in their field.

  • A team of graduate students from the computer science program recently placed highly in a national cybersecurity contest

    The CyberSalukis achieved an impressive 16th place overall in the U.S. Department of Energy’s CyberForce Competition, held in late 2022. This national competition saw participation from over 140 teams representing 108 institutions from across the country. The SIU School of Computing's CyberSalukis team distinguished themselves by securing 4th place among the 12 participating teams from Illinois. Additionally, another SIU team, the Security Dawgs, demonstrated commendable performance by finishing 41st overall in this highly competitive event.

  • Outstanding Reviewer Award in AutomotiveUI Conference

    The AutomotiveUI Conference is a highly esteemed event in the field of automotive user interface and user experience research. It brings together leading experts, researchers, and practitioners from around the globe to discuss the latest advancements and innovations in automotive technology. Receiving the Outstanding Reviewer Award at such a prestigious conference is a significant honor, recognizing an individual's exceptional contribution to the peer review process. This award highlights the recipient's expertise, meticulous attention to detail, and dedication to advancing the field. It is a mark of distinction that underscores the recipient's role in shaping high-quality, impactful research within the automotive UI community.
