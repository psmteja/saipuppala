Introduction
Sai Puppala is a Ph.D. student at Southern Illinois University Carbondale and a member of the Data Science team at a leading corporate organization. His expertise includes Big Data, Machine Learning, Parallel Processing, and Cloud Services. Previously, as a Data Scientist, he contributed to notable projects such as Google CP. With extensive experience across various domains—including Financial Services, Retail, Cloud Services, Transportation, and Manufacturing—he holds certifications as a Solutions Architect and Data Engineer. His passion for Big Data and Machine Learning drives him to continuously explore innovations in these fields. Proficient in multiple programming languages and technologies, Sai's skill set encompasses C, C++, Java, .NET, Python, Hive, Hadoop, Spark, NoSQL, SQL Server, Oracle, R, Impala, HBase, SAS, Tableau, and Perl. He has also earned certifications in Big Data Analytics from esteemed institutions such as Columbia University, MIT, Harvard University, Microsoft, and IIT Hyderabad. His research focuses on the intersection of Big Data, Networking, Security, and the adaptability of Machine Learning. He aims to address challenges at the convergence of Big Data and Security through advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning methodologies, ensuring effective real-world applications.
